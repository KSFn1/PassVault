# **PassVault**

A fully functional and secure credential management Windows application that uses advanced encryption to protect user data. It also provides an intuitive, user friendly interface for a seamless experience.

---

## **Login Page**

The login page contains several buttons that serve different purposes.

### **Existing Users**
If you already have an account:

1. Enter your **username** and **password**.  
2. You can use the **eye icon** to toggle password visibility (this is true for all eye icons in the program).  
3. Click **Login** to proceed.

### **Forgot Your Password?**
If you have forgotten your password, click **“Reset here”**.  
This will send a verification email to the email address you registered with.  
A detailed explanation of the password reset process is described further in this document.

### **New Users**
If you do not have an account, click **“Create one here”** to begin registration.

### **Please Note**
For the safety and security of our users, PassVault utilizes DPAPI to encrypt and protect your credentials within our cloud database. As a result, your account can only be accessed from the device on which it was originally created, ensuring layers of protection.

## **Register Page**

The register page allows you to create a new account using:

- Email address  
- Username  
- Password  

### **Steps to Register**
1. Enter your **email** in the *Email* box. Keep in mind that the entered email must be in the proper format (eg. xyz@gmail.com). 
2. Enter your **username** in the *Username* box.  
3. Create a password in the *Password* box.
4. Note: Duplicate emails/usernames are not allowed.

### **Password Requirements**
All passwords must:
- Be longer than **8 characters**
- Include at least **one uppercase letter**
- Include at least **one lowercase letter**
- Include at least **one symbol**

4. Re-enter your password in the *Re-enter Password* box.  
5. All four fields must be filled:
   - Email  
   - Username  
   - Password  
   - Re-enter Password  

Once everything is filled in correctly, click **Register**.

The **Back** button (present throughout the app) always returns you to the previous page without saving any changes.

---

## **Reset Password Page**

The Reset Password page allows you to reset your account password.

### **Steps**
1. Enter your email address in the **Email** box.  
2. Click **Send Email** to receive a verification code.  
3. You will receive an email from **passvaultms@gmail.com**.

4. Inside the app, enter the **6-digit code** from the email and click **Verify**.

### **Setting a New Password**
After verification, enter your new password in:
- **New Password**
- **Re-enter Password**

Then choose:
- **RESET** to save the new password  
- **CANCEL** to keep your existing password

## **Credentials Storage**

### **Adding Passwords**
Use the **“Add”** button to add your credentials as an entry.

You will be prompted to enter:
- Platform  
- Username  
- Password  
- Email  
- Website URL 

---

### **Editing Passwords**
On the right hand side, select the row of credentials that you want to edit.
Click the **Edit** button to modify an existing entry.  
You will be prompted to update:
- Platform  
- Username  
- Password  
- Email  
- Website URL

If you intend not to edit all previously entred data, press "OK" to move to the next entry.

Keep in mind that pressing "Cancel" makes the selected entry empty.

---

## **Search Function**

The search bar allows you to filter entries by platform name, username, email address, and website URL.

Typing part of those will display entries that most closely match your search.

**Example:**  
Searching **“App”** will show all platforms beginning with “App,” sorted from closest to least similar.

## **Contact Us Page**

For any PassVault-related inquiries:

1. Press the **Contact Us** button  
2. Enter your **name**  
3. Enter your **email**  
4. Briefly describe your issue or question  
5. Click **Submit**

You will receive a response through the email you entered.

---

## **Logout**

The red logout button can be used to fully exit the app. 

When pressed, a dialogue box with two options appears: "Yes" and "No".

If "Yes" is chosen, the app will fully close and all information used during login will get erased. Therefore, when the next session is started, username and password is required again.

If "No" is chosen, the user will be back where they left off before pressing the "Logout" button.

# **PassVault – User Guide**

---

## **Login Page**

The login page contains several buttons that serve different purposes.

### **Existing Users**
If you already have an account:

1. Enter your **username** and **password**.  
2. You can use the **eye icon** to toggle password visibility (this is true for all eye icons in the program).  
3. Click **Login** to proceed.

### **Forgot Your Password?**
If you have forgotten your password, click **“Reset here”**.  
This will send a verification email to the email address you registered with.  
A detailed explanation of the password reset process is described further in this document.

### **New Users**
If you do not have an account, click **“Create one here”** to begin registration.

---

## **Register Page**

The register page allows you to create a new account using:

- Email address  
- Username  
- Password  

### **Steps to Register**
1. Enter your **email** in the *Email* box.  
2. Enter your **username** in the *Username* box.  
3. Create a password in the *Password* box.

### **Password Requirements**
All passwords must:
- Be longer than **8 characters**
- Include at least **one uppercase letter**
- Include at least **one lowercase letter**
- Include at least **one symbol**

4. Re-enter your password in the *Re-enter Password* box.  
5. All four fields must be filled:
   - Email  
   - Username  
   - Password  
   - Re-enter Password  

Once everything is filled in correctly, click **Register**.

The **Back** button (present throughout the app) always returns you to the previous page.

---

## **Reset Password Page**

The Reset Password page allows you to reset your account password.

### **Steps**
1. Enter your email address in the **Email** box.  
2. Click **Send Email** to receive a verification code.  
3. You will receive an email from **passvaultms@gmail.com**.

4. Inside the app, enter the **6-digit code** from the email and click **Verify**.

### **Setting a New Password**
After verification, enter your new password in:
- **New Password**
- **Re-enter Password**

Then choose:
- **RESET** to save the new password  
- **CANCEL** to keep your existing password  

---

## **Password Storage**

### **Adding Passwords**
Use the **“Add”** button to add a password entry.

You will be prompted to enter:
- Platform  
- Website  
- Username  
- Password  
- Email  

---

### **Editing Passwords**
Click the **Edit** button to modify an existing entry.  
Click on the entry you want to edit, and you will be prompted to update:
- Platform  
- Username  
- Password  
- Email  
- Website  

---

## **Search Function**

The search bar allows you to filter entries by platform name.

Typing part of a platform name will display entries that most closely match your search.

**Example:**  
Searching **“App”** will show all platforms beginning with “App,” sorted from closest to least similar.

---

## **Contact Us Page**

For any PassVault-related inquiries:

1. Press the **Contact Us** button  
2. Enter your **name**  
3. Enter your **email**  
4. Briefly describe your issue or question  
5. Click **Submit**

You will receive a response through the email you entered.
